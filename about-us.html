The Origin Of The Story

Founded in 2014 in Saudi Arabia, RMAX began with a simple vision: to transform spaces and enhance lives through the power of light. From our roots in the Kingdom, we’ve grown into an international brand, delivering innovative lighting and electrical solutions that blend technology, design, and sustainability.

Our China-based factory and strategic offices in Hong Kong, India, UAE, Qatar, Bahrain, Oman, and Egypt allow us to serve a global network while staying true to our commitment to quality, affordability, and performance.

We believe that lighting is more than illumination—it shapes environments, influences well-being, and enhances architectural beauty. That’s why we create solutions that combine aesthetic design, energy efficiency, and human-centered technology.

Customer - Oriented

At RMAX, customer satisfaction is at the core of everything we do. We partner with distributors, developers, and businesses to provide reliable, innovative, and tailored solutions that exceed expectations. Our goal is to deliver value while building long-term relationships.

People - Centered Lighting & Electrical Solutions

Our human-centric approach drives the development of indoor and outdoor lighting, smart IoT solutions, and wiring devices. Whether it's chandeliers, streetlights, bollards, switches, or sockets, every RMAX product is designed to improve comfort, productivity, and lifestyle.

Forward Momentum

Our customers are our greatest motivation. We've proudly completed projects across residential, commercial, hospitality, and industrial sectors, supporting smart cities, luxury spaces, and sustainable developments across Saudi Arabia, UAE, Qatar, Bahrain, Oman, India, China, Egypt, and beyond.

Our Professional Team

Behind RMAX is a dedicated team of engineers, designers, and R&D professionals, driven by innovation and excellence. Their expertise ensures that every product meets international standards, blending modern design, reliable function, and sustainability.

Our Future Outlook

Our ambition is to expand globally, delivering smarter, energy-efficient solutions to more markets. We aim to be a trusted partner in lighting, energy management, and automation, offering affordable innovation for homes, businesses, and large-scale projects worldwide.

RMAX is a leading innovator in the lighting industry, specializing in smart lighting solutions, energy-efficient systems, and modern electrical products.
